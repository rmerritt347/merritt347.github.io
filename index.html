Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) Safety Training

This interactive eLearning module was designed for industrial maintenance workers to master OSHA-compliant LOTO procedures. The course includes real-world simulations, layered instruction, knowledge checks, and a printable reference guide. Developed in Articulate Storyline, the course simulates high-stakes decision-making and reinforces procedural safety using multimedia and learner interaction.
