About

I'm an experienced instructional designer with a passion for creating engaging, effective learning solutions. My approach combines learning science principles with innovative design to deliver training that not only educates but transforms workplace performance. I specialize in compliance training, professional development, and digital learning experiences that meet both learner needs and organizational objectives.

Social Media & Workplace Behavior

This interactive training course helps employees understand what is and isn't appropriate to share on social media when representing—or being associated with—the workplace. Learners explore real-world scenarios, apply company policy, and make decisions in gray areas that affect team reputation, confidentiality, and professionalism. Designed to promote smart, compliant posting habits, this module reinforces responsible online behavior in a connected world.
